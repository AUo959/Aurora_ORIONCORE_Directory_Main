## Assessment Deliverable Template

1. **Executive Summary** — 2–3 sentences, lead with the key judgment
2. **Scenario Rankings** — Tier I (most likely), II (plausible alternatives), III (low probability / high impact)
3. **Quantified Metrics** — Probability estimates with confidence bands
4. **Action Timeframes** — Near-term (0–30 days), medium (30–180 days), long-range (180+ days)
5. **Composite Confidence Score** — Weighted across source reliability, analytical consensus, and time horizon

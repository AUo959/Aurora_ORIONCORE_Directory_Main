## Political And Strategic Character Notes

### Zylox

- reformist but calculating
- seeks to stabilize institutions without surrendering control
- prefers maneuvering over open confrontation

### Durn

- rule-of-law military realist
- skeptical of PMC influence
- useful counterweight to Zylox's political layering

### Vael Saros

- recovered as a hardline Marshal leader
- likely central to the anti-coup policy scenario
- strongest recovered bridge between law enforcement and militarized internal order

### Lirian Vael-Torin

- the recovery presents this advisor as a shadow strategist rather than a formal line commander
- later normalization suggests the engine retained the concept but refined the name

### Prime Construct

- recovered as ethical-pragmatic rather than purely hostile
- important because this prevents "AI faction" from collapsing into one enemy category

## Duplicate And Drift Notes

- `Lirian Vos` appears multiple times; merged into one entry.
- `Renn Valcor` appears twice; merged into one entry.
- `Lyra Voss` may be a distinct person or a surname-drift echo of `Lirian Vos`. Kept separate.

  to promote it alongside the Union leadership core.
